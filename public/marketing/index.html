About Tabata Welfare Association

Founded in 2016, Tabata Welfare Association has been at the forefront of community support in Tanzania. We believe that together, we can weather any storm and celebrate every success.

Our mission is simple: to provide financial security and peace of mind to our members through collective contributions, disaster support, and community solidarity.

Financial Security

Monthly contributions build a safety net for all members, ensuring financial stability during challenging times.

Community Support

We stand together in times of need and celebration, creating lasting bonds that strengthen our community.

Trusted Leadership

Transparent management with member-focused governance ensures accountability and trust in all our operations.

Our Services

Comprehensive welfare services designed to support our members through all of life's moments

Monthly Contributions

Regular monthly contributions of 20,000 TZS build a collective fund that ensures financial security for all members.

  • Flexible payment options
  • Automatic reminders
  • Transparent tracking

Disaster Support

Immediate financial assistance during emergencies, medical crises, or unexpected life events that affect our members.

  • Emergency medical support
  • Family crisis assistance
  • Quick disbursement

Family Coverage

Extend welfare benefits to your dependents including spouse, children, and immediate family members.

  • Spouse coverage
  • Children protection
  • Extended family support

Financial Planning

Professional guidance on financial planning, savings strategies, and long-term wealth building for members.

  • Personal consultations
  • Investment advice
  • Retirement planning

Community Events

Regular social gatherings, educational workshops, and community building activities for all members.

  • Monthly meetings
  • Educational workshops
  • Social celebrations

Digital Platform

Modern online platform for managing contributions, tracking benefits, and staying connected with the community.

  • Online dashboard
  • Mobile-friendly
  • Real-time updates
